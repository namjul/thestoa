January 30th, 2022

Join Peter for The Stoa’s first bookless club - where we enjoy blogs, essays, video essays, and films, followed by having a delicious dialogos together.

The first session will be about Tiqqun’s “terrible community,” along with other failure modes of communing, e.g. “tyranny of structureless” and “culty cults.” These readings are meant to tease out some theories that can help us develop the praxis of Social Alchemy.
